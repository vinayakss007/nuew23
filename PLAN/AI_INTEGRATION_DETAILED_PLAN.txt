────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 MODULE 1: AI LEAD SCORING (HOT / WARM / COLD)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT IS:
  Every contact in the CRM gets a score from 0-100 and a label:
  - HOT (80-100): Ready to buy, high engagement, matches ideal customer
  - WARM (50-79): Interested but needs nurturing
  - COLD (0-49): Low engagement, wrong fit, or not ready

WHO DOES THIS TODAY & MAKES MONEY:

  1. HubSpot (Predictive Lead Scoring)
     - Part of Enterprise plan ($3,200/month)
     - Uses ML on 200+ data points
     - Scores contacts AND companies
     - Shows WHY a score was given (explainable AI)
     - Proven: 30% increase in conversion rate for users

  2. Salesforce (Einstein Lead Scoring)
     - Part of Einstein AI add-on ($75/user/month)
     - Scores based on historical won/lost deals
     - Auto-adjusts as more data comes in
     - Proven: 41% increase in qualified leads

  3. Zoho (Zia Lead Scoring)
     - Part of Zoho CRM Plus (₹8,000/user/month)
     - Uses behavioral, demographic, and social signals
     - Proven: 25% reduction in sales cycle

  4. Pipedrive (Lead Scoring)
     - Part of Professional plan ($49.90/user/month)
     - Simple rules-based scoring (not AI)
     - Proves that EVEN simple scoring sells

KEY INSIGHT: HubSpot charges $3,200/month for this feature. We can offer it
for ₹999/month as part of our Starter plan because GPT-4o-mini is extremely
cheap. This is a MASSIVE competitive advantage.

HOW IT WORKS TECHNICALLY:

  INPUT DATA (from our database):
    - Contact info: email domain, phone area code, company name
    - Activity data: email opens, clicks, replies, page visits
    - Deal data: deal value, stage, days in pipeline
    - Behavioral data: last activity date, activity frequency
    - Historical data: did similar contacts convert before?

  AI PROMPT (what we send to OpenAI):
    System: "You are an expert B2B sales analyst. Score this lead 0-100
    based on likelihood to close. Consider: engagement level, company fit,
    deal stage, recency, and historical patterns. Return ONLY JSON."

    User: JSON with contact data, activities, deal info, company info

  AI RESPONSE:
    {
      "score": 87,
      "label": "HOT",
      "confidence": 0.91,
      "factors": {
        "positive": ["Opened proposal 3 times", "Reply within 2 hours", "Decision maker"],
        "negative": ["Small company (5 employees)", "No budget confirmed"],
        "neutral": ["First time buyer"]
      },
      "next_best_action": "Call today — they're actively reviewing your proposal",
      "estimated_close_probability": 0.78,
      "estimated_close_days": 7
    }

  COST PER SCORE:
    - GPT-4o-mini: ~₹0.003 per score (3 tokens input, 100 tokens output)
    - 1,000 scores = ₹3
    - 10,000 scores = ₹30
    - This is practically FREE

GLOBAL USAGE PROOF:
  - 73% of top-performing sales teams use lead scoring (Forrester)
  - Lead scoring increases revenue by 10-15% (Marketing Sherpa)
  - 68% of buyers expect immediate response (Sprout Social)
  - AI-scored leads convert 4.2x better than random leads (InsideSales)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 MODULE 2: AI REVENUE AGENT (Automated Follow-up System)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT IS:
  Never lose a deal to silence. The system automatically:
  - Detects when a deal needs follow-up
  - Generates personalized follow-up content
  - Sends via email or WhatsApp
  - Tracks engagement (opens, clicks, replies)
  - Escalates to human if no response

WHO DOES THIS TODAY:

  1. HubSpot (Sequences + Workflows)
     - Automated email sequences with personalization
     - Part of Professional plan ($890/month)
     - Proven: 45% more emails sent by sales teams

  2. Outreach.io (Sales Engagement Platform)
     - Pure follow-up automation platform
     - $100/user/month
     - $300M ARR company
     - Proven: 3x more meetings booked

  3. Salesloft
     - Similar to Outreach
     - $125/user/month
     - Proven: 30% increase in pipeline

KEY INSIGHT: Outreach.io is a $300M company built ENTIRELY around follow-up
automation. We're building this as ONE MODULE inside our ₹2,999/month plan.

HOW IT WORKS TECHNICALLY:

  STEP 1: Detection (every 30 minutes)
    Query: Find deals that need follow-up
    SELECT * FROM deals
    WHERE (metadata->>'revenue_next_followup')::timestamp < NOW()
    AND stage NOT IN ('won', 'lost')
    LIMIT 50

  STEP 2: Content Generation (per deal)
    Gather context: contact name, deal value, previous interactions, AI score
    Generate email: "Hi {name}, following up on {deal}..."
    Cost: ₹0.01 per email (GPT-4o-mini)

  STEP 3: Delivery
    Send via Resend API (email) or WhatsApp API
    Track with pixel + redirect links

  STEP 4: Engagement Tracking
    If opened → log event, re-score, decide next action
    If clicked → log event, notify salesperson
    If no response in 48h → escalate to next follow-up

  STEP 5: Escalation Ladder
    Follow-up 1: Email (AI-generated) — Day 0
    Follow-up 2: Email (different angle) — Day 3
    Follow-up 3: WhatsApp message — Day 5
    Follow-up 4: Task for human to call — Day 7
    Follow-up 5: "Deal going cold" alert to manager — Day 10

  COST PER FOLLOW-UP:
    - Email: ₹0.001 (nearly free with Resend free tier)
    - WhatsApp: ₹0.50-2.00 per message
    - AI generation: ₹0.01 per follow-up
    - Average cost: ₹0.05-0.50 per follow-up

GLOBAL USAGE PROOF:
  - 80% of sales require 5+ follow-up calls after the meeting (Marketing Donut)
  - 44% of salespeople give up after 1 follow-up (HubSpot)
  - Automated follow-up increases response rate by 300% (Yesware)
  - Companies using automated follow-up see 27% higher win rates (Salesforce)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 MODULE 3: WHATSAPP AI CLOSER (WhatsApp Sales Agent)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT IS:
  An AI that replies to WhatsApp messages from leads/customers on behalf of
  the sales team. It:
  - Understands context (previous conversations, deal stage, contact score)
  - Handles objections naturally
  - Sends pricing, proposals, case studies
  - Books meetings
  - Escalates to human when needed
  - Supports Hindi, Marathi, English (code-switching)
  - Handles voice notes (speech-to-text → AI reply → text or voice)

WHO DOES THIS TODAY:

  1. Wati (WhatsApp Team Inbox + AI)
     - ₹3,500/month + WhatsApp charges
     - Team inbox with AI assist
     - No full AI automation yet

  2. Interakt (WhatsApp + Shopify)
     - ₹1,500/month
     - Catalog sharing, automated messages
     - Basic AI, not conversational

  3. Gupshup (WhatsApp Business API Partner)
     - Enterprise pricing
     - Bot builder for WhatsApp
     - Used by large Indian companies

  4. ManyChat (WhatsApp Automation)
     - $15/month
     - Flow builder (not conversational AI)
     - Popular but limited to pre-defined flows

KEY INSIGHT: NO ONE is doing FULL AI conversational sales on WhatsApp yet.
Everyone is doing template-based or flow-based automation. A truly AI-powered
WhatsApp sales agent that understands context and handles objections is a
BLUE OCEAN market — especially in India.

HOW IT WORKS TECHNICALLY:

  WHATSAPP BUSINESS API SETUP:
    1. Tenant needs Meta Business account (business.facebook.com)
    2. Gets WhatsApp Business API access through:
       Option A: Meta Cloud API (direct — cheapest, no BSP markup)
       Option B: BSP like Gupshup/Interakt (easier setup, more expensive)
    3. We recommend Meta CloudAPI for cost efficiency

  META CLOUD API DETAILS:
    Base URL: https://graph.facebook.com/v18.0/{phone_number_id}/messages
    Auth: Bearer token (access_token)
    Webhook: Meta sends incoming messages to our server

  WEBHOOK HANDLER:
    POST /api/webhooks/whatsapp
    Verifies: x-hub-signature-256 header
    Parses: message object (text, image, audio, document, location)
    Routes: to WhatsApp AI Agent

  AI AGENT FLOW:
    1. Receive incoming message
    2. Find contact by phone number (or create new)
    3. Load context:
       - Last 10 messages in conversation
       - Contact's AI score
       - Active deals
       - Previous interactions
       - Company info
    4. Build AI prompt with full context
    5. Generate response
    6. Send via WhatsApp API
    7. Store message in database
    8. Log activity

  VOICE NOTE HANDLING:
    1. Receive audio message (ogg/opus)
    2. Download from WhatsApp media URL
    3. Send to OpenAI Whisper API
    4. Get text transcription
    5. Process as normal text message
    6. AI generates text response
    7. Send text (or convert to voice with TTS)

  MULTI-LANGUAGE:
    - Detect language from incoming message
    - Set AI system prompt language accordingly
    - Supports: Hindi, Marathi, English, Hinglish (code-switching)
    - AI adapts to user's language automatically

  MESSAGE CATEGORIES (WhatsApp pricing impact):
    Service (FREE): User messages us → 24h window → all replies free
    Utility (₹0.35-1.00): Receipts, updates, confirmations
    Marketing (₹0.70-2.00): Promotional, follow-ups, campaigns
    Authentication (₹0.35-0.70): OTP, verification

  COST OPTIMIZATION:
    - Design flows to use FREE service window
    - User messages first → 24h free window opens
    - All AI replies within window = FREE
    - Only chargeable: proactive outbound (marketing)

  COST PER CONVERSATION:
    - AI response: ₹0.30
    - WhatsApp (within service window): FREE
    - WhatsApp (marketing outbound): ₹0.70-2.00
    - Voice transcription: ₹0.50/min
    - Average conversation: ₹0.30-2.30

GLOBAL USAGE PROOF:
  - 2 billion+ WhatsApp users worldwide
  - 98% open rate for WhatsApp messages (vs 20% email)
  - 60% of Indian SMBs use WhatsApp as primary business tool
  - WhatsApp business messaging grew 3x in India (2024-2025)
  - AI chatbots on WhatsApp see 40% higher engagement than web chat

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 MODULE 5: AI VOICE CALL AGENT (Twilio + AI)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WHAT IT IS:
  AI makes/receives phone calls to qualify leads, handle objections, and
  book meetings. Full conversation with human-like responses.

WHO DOES THIS TODAY:

  1. Bland AI (bland.ai)
     - $0.12-0.30/min
     - Full AI phone calls
     - Pre-built call agents
     - Fastest to implement

  2. Vapi.ai
     - $0.06-0.12/min
     - Real-time AI voice calls
     - Developer-friendly API
     - Custom voice/personality

  3. Retell AI
     - $0.05-0.10/min
     - Conversational AI for calls
     - Low latency

  4. Twilio + OpenAI Realtime API
     - DIY approach
     - Twilio: ₹1.40/min (India)
     - OpenAI Realtime: $0.06/min
     - Most flexible but most complex

  5. Synthflow AI
     - $29/month + usage
     - No-code AI phone agent

  6. Air AI
     - $399/month
     - Enterprise AI caller

KEY INSIGHT: Bland AI and Vapi are the easiest to integrate. They handle
the entire call flow — we just define the prompt and get callbacks.
Twilio + OpenAI Realtime is cheapest but requires building the entire
audio streaming pipeline.

HOW IT WORKS:

  EASIEST APPROACH (Bland AI or Vapi):
    1. Register on platform
    2. Define AI agent prompt
    3. Provide phone number (or use theirs)
    4. Call API: { phone_number, task: "qualify this lead", ... }
    5. Platform handles the entire call
    6. Webhook sends: transcript, summary, recording URL
    7. We store in voice_calls table

  COST (Bland AI):
    - $0.12-0.30/min = ₹10-25/min
    - Average 3-min call = ₹30-75
    - We charge customer: ₹3-5/min → margin of ₹1-2/min

  COST (Vapi):
    - $0.06-0.12/min = ₹5-10/min
    - Average 3-min call = ₹15-30
    - We charge customer: ₹3-5/min → higher margin

  COST (Twilio + OpenAI Realtime):
    - Twilio India outbound: ₹1.40/min
    - OpenAI Realtime API: $0.06/min = ₹5/min
    - Total: ₹6.40/min
    - Average 3-min call = ₹19.20
    - We charge customer: ₹3-5/min → LOWEST cost but HIGHEST complexity

  RECOMMENDATION: Start with Vapi.ai — best balance of cost, ease, and
  quality. Migrate to Twilio + OpenAI Realtime at scale for lower costs.

GLOBAL USAGE PROOF:
  - AI voice agents market: $1.6B in 2024, growing 23% YoY
  - 60% of consumers prefer voice calls for complex inquiries
  - AI voice agents handle 80% of routine calls without human transfer
  - Average cost savings: 40-60% vs human agents
