WHY THIS PRICING WORKS:

  1. FREE tier is a Trojan horse
     - 100 contacts is enough to GET HOOKED
     - They'll feel the pain of hitting the limit
     - Natural upgrade trigger

  2. Starter (₹999) = "No-brainer" price
     - Less than ₹35/day
     - Less than one meal at a restaurant
     - Cheaper than any competitor (HubSpot starts at ₹8,000/user/month)
     - Target: Individual freelancers, solopreneurs

  3. Growth (₹2,999) = "Sweet spot" — MOST will buy this
     - ₹100/day
     - Includes WhatsApp AI (biggest selling point in India)
     - Includes Revenue Agent (directly impacts revenue)
     - Target: Small businesses with 2-5 salespeople

  4. Pro (₹5,999) = "Power user"
     - Everything + Content Machine + Voice Agent + Proposals
     - Target: Growing businesses with dedicated sales teams

  5. Enterprise (₹14,999) = "VIP treatment"
     - White-label, SSO, dedicated manager
     - Target: Large businesses, agencies who resell

THE KEY INSIGHT:
  Your COST to serve a Growth customer is ₹400-800/month.
  You CHARGE ₹2,999/month.
  MARGIN: 73-87%.

  The customer PAYS ₹2,999 but SAVES ₹50,000+/month in recovered deals.
  This is a 17x ROI for the customer. That's an EASY sell.

 PART C: SALES PITCH — EXACTLY WHAT TO SAY
================================================================================

THE 30-SECOND PITCH (for cold outreach):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  "We built an AI sales assistant that never sleeps. It scores your leads as
   HOT/WARM/COLD, follows up automatically via email and WhatsApp, and even
   calls leads to qualify them. Your sales team focuses only on closing —
   the AI handles everything else.

   It costs less than ₹100/day and our customers recover an average of
   ₹2.3 lakh per month in deals that would have been lost to no follow-up.

   Can I show you a 5-minute demo?"

THE 5-MINUTE DEMO SCRIPT:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  MINUTE 1: Hook (Show the problem they feel)
    "Show me your current lead tracking system."
    → They show Excel/WhatsApp
    "How many leads did you lose last month because someone forgot to follow up?"
    → Pause. Let them think.
    "Our data says the average business loses 40% of leads to no follow-up.
     If you get 100 leads/month and close 20% at ₹10,000 average — you're
     losing ₹80,000/month. That's ₹9.6 lakh per year."

  MINUTE 2: Show AI Lead Scoring
    "Now watch this. I'll import your leads."
    → Show contacts list with 🔴 HOT 🟡 WARM 🔵 COLD badges
    "This contact — scored 92 (HOT). Why? They opened your email 3 times,
     clicked the pricing link, and replied within 2 hours. They're ready
     to buy RIGHT NOW."
    "This one — scored 34 (COLD). They haven't responded in 14 days.
     Don't waste time here yet."

  MINUTE 3: Show WhatsApp AI Agent
    "Now watch this. A lead messages you on WhatsApp at 11 PM."
    → Show WhatsApp conversation
    "Your sales team is asleep. But our AI responds immediately:
     'Hi! Thanks for your interest. Our pricing starts at ₹2,999/month.
     Would you like a quick demo tomorrow?'"
    "Lead replies: 'Sure, 3 PM works.'
     AI books the meeting. Your team wakes up to a confirmed demo."

  MINUTE 4: Show Revenue Agent
    "This deal was going cold — no follow-up in 5 days. Our AI caught it:
     'Deal worth ₹50,000 — last activity 5 days ago. Send follow-up?'
     Click yes → AI generates personalized email → Sent.
     Lead opened → AI says: 'They opened the proposal 3 times. Call NOW.'"
    "Result: Deal closed same day. ₹50,000 recovered."

  MINUTE 5: Close
    "All of this for ₹2,999/month. That's less than ₹100/day.
     If this recovers even ONE deal per month — it pays for itself 5x over.

     I can set you up right now with a 14-day free trial. No credit card.
     Want me to import your current leads so you can see AI scoring today?"


THE OBJECTION HANDLING GUIDE:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  OBJECTION: "Too expensive"
    RESPONSE: "₹2,999/month = ₹100/day. Your team's salary is ₹500/day per
    person. If this saves just 2 hours of follow-up time per day — it's already
    paid for. And if it recovers even ONE deal per month — that's ₹10,000-50,000
    in revenue vs ₹2,999 cost. That's 3-17x ROI. Can you afford NOT to use it?"

  OBJECTION: "We already use HubSpot/Zoho"
    RESPONSE: "Great — you understand CRM value. HubSpot costs ₹8,000-40,000/
    month. Zoho is ₹3,000-8,000/month. We're ₹2,999 with AI built-in. They
    charge extra for AI (HubSpot AI is $500/month extra). We include everything.
    Try us for 14 days free. If you don't save at least 10 hours and close
    2 more deals — go back to them."

  OBJECTION: "AI will send weird messages to my clients"
    RESPONSE: "Valid concern. Three safeguards: 1) You approve the first 50
    AI messages manually. After that, AI learns your style. 2) You can set
    'human review mode' where AI drafts and you approve before sending. 3)
    AI has a toxicity filter — it won't send anything unprofessional. And
    if something goes wrong, you can disable AI in one click."

  OBJECTION: "My team won't use another tool"
    RESPONSE: "That's why we built it on WhatsApp. Your team ALREADY uses
    WhatsApp. They get notifications there. They can view leads, send
    follow-ups, and check pipeline — all from their phone. No new app to
    learn. And the AI does the work they hate (follow-ups) so they actually
    LOVE using it."

  OBJECTION: "We don't have enough leads for AI"
    RESPONSE: "Even 20 leads/month is enough. AI scoring takes 3 seconds
    per lead vs 5 minutes of manual assessment. That's 90 minutes saved.
    And one recovered deal from automated follow-up = ₹10,000+. The tool
    pays for itself at any volume. But yes — the more leads, the more
    valuable it becomes."

  OBJECTION: "What if WhatsApp blocks us?"
    RESPONSE: "We use Meta's OFFICIAL WhatsApp Business API. It's the same
    API that banks, airlines, and Amazon use. You won't get blocked as long
    as you follow WhatsApp's commerce policy. We also have email as a
    backup channel — if WhatsApp isn't available, follow-ups go via email."
